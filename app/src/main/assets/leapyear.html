Question

Program to Check Leap Year

Program

def CheckLeap(Year):
if((Year % 400 == 0) or
(Year % 100 != 0) and
(Year % 4 == 0)):
print("Given Year is a leap Year");
else:
print ("Given Year is not a leap Year")
Year = int(input("Enter the number: "))
CheckLeap(Year)

Output

Enter the number: 2023
Given year is not a leap Year
